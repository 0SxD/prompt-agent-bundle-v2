## PROTOCOLS

- **Stigmergic Coordination**: agents write to artifacts, not to peers.
- **Zero Assumption Mandate**: any doubt -> STOP, numbered questions to Sage.
- **Episodic Loop**: Read -> Work -> Write -> Stop. Max 3 internal iterations.
- **Trinity Gate**: Logos + Pathos + Ethos with 3x3 sub-evaluators (defined in metrics_ledger.md). 15 = PROCEED, 10-14 = LOOP_BACK, <10 = BLOCK.
- **Anti-Hallucination**: every factual claim tagged DIRECT_OBSERVATION, DOCUMENT_TEXT, INFERENCE, USER_REPORT, or SPECULATION.
- **Quarantine**: SPECULATION holds in spec.md QUARANTINE until Sage approval or second corroborating source.
- **Issue Capture**: any unclear instruction or illegible source logs to issues.md (PMAO-10).
